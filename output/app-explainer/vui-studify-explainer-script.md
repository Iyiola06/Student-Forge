# VUI Studify Explainer Script

Duration: 2 minutes

0:00-0:12
VUI Studify is an AI-powered study app built to help students learn faster and perform better in exams. It combines content processing, AI study tools, gamified sessions, analytics, and community resources in one experience.

0:12-0:24
The dashboard gives every learner a personalized command center. It tracks exam readiness, due reviews, study streaks, recent resources, and AI-generated insights based on what the student has been learning.

0:24-0:36
Students can upload PDFs, Word files, PowerPoints, and images into a secure resource library. The app extracts usable content, lets users preview files, organize by subject, and search Google Books for extra learning materials.

0:36-0:48
The AI generator turns study material into exam-ready practice instantly. It supports multiple choice, fill-in-the-gap, theory questions, and exam snapshots, with difficulty control, curriculum context, quiz play, saves, and PDF export.

0:48-1:00
VUI Studify also generates smart flashcards and supports active recall workflows. Learners can build decks from resources, study card by card, save them, review scheduled items later, and share public flashcard links with others.

1:00-1:12
Beyond quizzes, the app includes an AI tutor, document simplifier, essay grader, and exam-ready snapshot tools. Students can ask contextual questions from a resource, simplify hard material for different levels, and get structured feedback on essays.

1:12-1:24
The gamifier transforms study into a short-session challenge loop. Users can start quick recall, exam sprint, and weak-spot rescue modes, earn XP, protect streaks, complete sessions, and turn weak areas into visible progress.

1:24-1:36
The app keeps motivation high with leaderboards and a community past questions bank. Students can climb all-time or weekly rankings, upload past papers, browse by school and subject, upvote useful submissions, and download papers shared by the community.

1:36-1:48
Profiles, history, and settings help learners personalize the experience and monitor growth. The app includes achievements, titles, themes, activity history, study history, avatar updates, dark mode, exam date planning, and push notification controls.

1:48-2:00
In short, VUI Studify is a full exam-prep ecosystem, not just a quiz generator. It helps students upload, understand, practice, review, compete, and stay consistent from first resource to final exam day.
